Yll Agimi, PhD, MS, MPH

I'm a Senior Data Scientist, specializing in healthcare delivery and health services research. My background is in Epidemiology and Data Science (Statistics and Research Methodology). I currently serve as a Presidential Innovation Fellow (PIF). Most recently I worked at General Dynamics Information Technology as a Senior Data Scientist, leading a team building advanced data solutions for clients, including the Military Health System (MHS), the health system supporting some 10 million active-duty personnel, military retirees and their families. Yll partnered with senior stakeholders to scope, develop and deliver new innovative approaches to incorporating data science into aspects of healthcare operations and clinical decision making. He oversaw the development and use of algorithms and statistical predictive models to evaluate health outcomes. He led teams in determining analytical approaches and modeling techniques. Supported clients in extracting insights out of structured and unstructured data to solve complex business problems by relying on statistical techniques and broad knowledge of healthcare systems. I have studied and published on healthcare utilizaiton and injury patterns among civilian and military populations as well as the impact of state policies on injury rates. I led the standing up of the military Traumatic Brain Injury (TBI) surveillance system. Prior work included studying the role of state helmet laws on motorcycle-related TBI rates among young drivers, the role of mandated physician reporting of at-risk drivers on dementia-related vehicle crashes and the evaluation of military behavioral health program portfolio. At the Office of the Assistant Secretary of Defense (ASD) for Health Affairs, I led the development of the Department of Defense Medical Manpower Transparency System. This first-of-its-kind system integrates medical personnel data from four human resource systems (Army, Navy, Air Force, and Civilian) with financial planning and workload tracking systems. It provides ASD level leaders with enterprise-wide visibility into medical personnel distribution, fill rates by Service, provider, clinic type and location, staffing gaps, and nearby available providers. I also spearheaded the creation of a tri-Service + civilian intranet for data sharing, designed reporting metrics, and led the refinement of civilian medical profession coding.
